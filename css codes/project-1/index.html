senery of kashmir

Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is a land of unmatched natural beauty nestled in the majestic Himalayas. Its snow-covered peaks, emerald green valleys, and crystal-clear rivers create a breathtaking landscape. The iconic Dal Lake, with its floating houseboats and vibrant shikaras, offers a peaceful and picturesque view. In spring, tulip gardens bloom with color, while winter turns the region into a snowy wonderland. Dense pine forests, rolling meadows like Gulmarg and Pahalgam, and the soothing sound of flowing streams make Kashmir a dream destination. Its peaceful charm, scenic beauty, and refreshing climate leave every visitor truly mesmerized.
